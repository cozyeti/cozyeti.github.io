ARTIST STATEMENT

My artwork takes a critical view of sensitive materials and topics. I enjoy incorporating distress/glitch into my pieces because it's visually appealing in my opinion. When working on new pieces, I try not to focus on how I perceive myself emotionally while working so that I am cozy and clear-headed. Often times I find myself exploring random techniques and methods to attain an accurate representation of my thoughts through art.
